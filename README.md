# Consumer-Shopping-Behavior-Analysis-using-Power-BI

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing consumer shopping behavior using Power BI to uncover valuable business insights related to customer purchasing patterns, product performance, marketing effectiveness, and customer engagement.

The analysis helps retail businesses understand how different factors such as demographics, discounts, product categories, seasons, reviews, and payment methods influence customer buying decisions.

The project aims to support data-driven decision-making by creating interactive dashboards and identifying actionable insights for improving sales performance and customer satisfaction.

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer shopping behavior data with transactional and demographic information.

- Total Rows: 3900
- Total Columns: 18

## Key Insights

- Clothing category generates the highest revenue among all product categories.
- 73% of customers are non-subscribers, indicating low membership conversion.
- The average customer purchase amount is $59.76 per transaction.
- Average customer review rating stands at 3.75, showing moderate satisfaction.
- Adult customers contribute the highest share of total revenue.
- Young customers generate the lowest revenue compared to other age groups.
